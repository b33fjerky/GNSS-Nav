1) Connect GSM Antenna, Earphones and Jumpers to B on Waveshare.

2) Insert SD Card in RPi and connect RPi to Power via PWR microUSB slot.

3) Attach the HAT on RPi GPIOS.

4) Connect Waveshare HAT to power via USBtoUART microUSB slot.

5) SSH into RPi via ssh pi@raspberrypi.local | password: raspberry

6) Use Python to initiate serial communication on port /dev/ttyS0

Done!
